STALKER GAMMA: Ammo Efficiency Analysis

Cheapest to buy, best to disassemble and all good craft conversion paths

Ammo crafting is around 90% efficient, meaning that for 10 bullets disassembled, you'll get, on average: 9 gunpowder, 9 casings and 9 bullet tips. The amount you get can vary widely though, so saying "by disassembling 2 boxes of this you can craft one box of that", while true on paper, might require more depending on your (bad) luck.

4. Good Crafts with Extra Loot

All the interesting ammo to craft when you have a ton of weaker rounds lying around.

📖
None of these crafts save money, every path below is a loss on paper. The point is turning ammo you looted and won't use into something more useful or powerful, at the smallest theoretical cost possible.
How to read the table
"3× 9x18 FMJ → 45ACP HYDRA · 18% overpay"
Means 3 boxes of 9x18 FMJ can craft 1 box of 45ACP HYDRA, at 18% above its regular purchase price.
2× 9x18 AP + 2× 9x18 FMJ → 5.7x28 SS190 · 35% overpay
Means 2 boxes of 9x18 AP AND 2 boxes of 9x18 FMJ can craft 1 box of 5.7x28 SS190, at 35% above its regular purchase price.
Only routes deemed "reasonable" are listed.
