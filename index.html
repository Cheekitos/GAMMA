Cheeki Numbers Mod
→ Get the mod here to help the project!
  • Install the mod at the bottom of your GAMMA modlist
  • Go to Main Menu → Mod Configuration Menu → Cheeki Numbers → Make sure Enable Mod and Enable File Log at the top are turned on.
  • Everything else can be turned off, the mod will run in the background without displaying anything in-game.
  • Your data will be saved automatically in YOUR DRIVE:\YOUR GAMMA FOLDER\overwrite\bin\cheeki_numbers.log
  • All your gaming sessions will be saved inside this single file, it does not get overwritten.
  • Send it to me at cheekitos@proton.me and make it to the leaderboard! NO MORE THAN ONE SUBMISSION PER WEEK PLEASE!
  • Make sure to rename the file or save it in a different folder, so next round of kills you send me is fresh and new.
  • (I recommend keeping your kill files, I have some cool extra features coming soon where you will be able to use them to track personal data)
⚠️
If you plan on sending me your data, please:
  • Do not use companions
  • Do not equip artifacts with on-hit effects
  • Avoid mods that modify the ballistic system (special ammo, weapons, etc.)
  • PLEASE DO NOT spawn squads just to mow them down.
  • Anything that will not reflect the damage your weapon is actually doing in real gameplay conditions defeats the purpose of the whole project.
  • AO Engine does not work with the mod, X-Ray Monolith Next Gen is ok, a bit buggy with mutants but I'll fix this soon!
