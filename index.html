Installation Guide
  • Install at the bottom of your modlist. The mod is for Vanilla GAMMA and I can't guarantee compatibility with extra addons.
  • The mod runs in the background without displaying anything in-game by default.
  • All the display options are opt-in and detailed in Mod Configuration Menu → Cheeki Numbers
  • Your data will be saved automatically in YOUR DRIVE:\YOUR GAMMA FOLDER\overwrite\bin\[cheeki_v1.32]_name_faction.log
  • All your gaming sessions will be saved inside this single log file, no need to do anything.
  • Submit your log file in the See my Stats section to help the rankings, community averages, and appear in the leaderboard.
  • You can submit the same log file multiple times as you progress the game. One submission max per week.
Important Information
🚩
If you plan on sending me your data, please:
  • Do not use mods that modify the vanilla ballistic system (special ammo types, reworked damage and resitances etc...)
  • Do not spawn squads just to mow them down
⚠️
Known Bugs & Limitations
  • AO Engine does not work with the mod
  • For any issues or bug reports you can contact me at cheekitos@proton.me - Please tell me:
    • → If you noticed what action broke the mod (killing a mutant, using a specific item...)
    • → If you're using the mod only silently in the background, or if it displays info on your screen
    • → If you're using any extra mods (Magazines etc..)
✔
  • You can use companions with the mod (kills that are not 100% yours will be skipped from the logs)
  • You can use perks artefacts with on-hit effects (kills that use them will be skipped from the logs)
  • Adding new / extra weapons is fine, as long as the mod you use doesn't rework the vanilla weapons
