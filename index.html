Get the mod here to help the project!
  • Install at the bottom of your GAMMA modlist
  • You can turn off everything in MCM except the last section (mod will run in the background)
  • Every time you're done playing and leave the game, go to YOUR DRIVE:\Your Anomaly Folder\appdata\logs
  • Rename the .log file so it doesn't get overwritten next time you launch the game
  • After gathering a couple files, drop them into the extractor tool above and send me the results at cheekitos@proton.me
  • Make it to the ranking pages and help finetune the best weapons rankings!
  • (Full guide can be found in this video)
⚠️
If you plan on sending me your data, please:
  • Do not use companions
  • Do not equip artifacts with on-hit effects
  • Avoid mods that modify the ballistic system (special ammo, weapons, etc.)
  • Spawn squads just to mow them down, etc. Anything that will not reflect the damage your weapon is actually doing in real gameplay conditions defeats the purpose of the whole project.
If you don't plan on sending me your logs ignore this :)
