Cheeki Numbers Mod
→ Get the mod here to help the project!
  • Install the mod at the bottom of your GAMMA modlist
  • Go to Main Menu → Mod Configuration Menu → Cheeki Numbers → Make sure Enable Mod and Enable File Log at the top are turned on.
  • Everything else can be turned off, the mod will run in the background without displaying anything in-game.
  • Your data will be saved automatically in YOUR DRIVE:\YOUR GAMMA FOLDER\overwrite\bin\cheeki_numbers_new.log
  • All your gaming sessions will be saved inside this single log file.
  • Submit your log file to help the rankings and appear in the leaderboard in the Submit Data & See my Stats section.
  • Rename the file or save it in a different folder after submission, so next round of kills you submit is fresh and new!
🚩
If you plan on sending me your data, please:
  • Do not use mods that modify the vanilla ballistic system (special ammo, weapon types etc...)
  • Do not equip artifacts with on-hit effects
  • PLEASE DO NOT spawn squads just to mow them down.
  • Anything that will not reflect the damage your weapon is actually doing in real gameplay conditions defeats the purpose of the whole project.
⚠️
Known Bugs & Limitations (rare, but if you encounter them turn off the mod until they're fixed)
  • Scopes turn black after mutant kill
  • Conflicts with Magazine mod and reloads
  • AO Engine does not work with the mod
  • For any other issues, old logs submissions or bug reports you can still contact me at cheekitos@proton.me
✔
  • You can use companions with the mod (kills that are not 100% yours will be skipped from the logs)
  • X-Ray Monolith Engine NextGen is compatible
